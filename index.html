F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about KrugerSSA safari experiences.

01

What animals can I expect to see on safari?

Our safaris cover diverse habitats in Kruger National Park, giving you the chance to see the Big Five — lions, elephants, rhinos, leopards, and buffalo — along with giraffes, zebras, hippos, and hundreds of bird species.

02

Are the safaris suitable for families with children?

Yes! We offer family-friendly safaris with safe, comfortable vehicles and activities tailored to engage children. Our guides ensure the experience is fun, educational, and memorable for all ages.

03

What is included in the safari packages?

Packages typically include guided game drives, park entry fees, accommodation at selected lodges, and meals. Luxury packages may also include private vehicles, fine dining, and exclusive experiences like night drives.

04

When is the best time to visit Kruger National Park?

The dry winter months (May–September) are ideal for wildlife viewing as animals gather near water sources. Summer (October–March) offers lush scenery and excellent birdwatching opportunities.

05

Do you offer customized safari experiences?

Absolutely. We tailor safaris to your preferences — whether you’re a photographer seeking the perfect shot, a couple looking for a romantic getaway, or a group wanting an adventure-packed itinerary.

About

Welcome to Kruger SA Safaris

At Kruger SA Safaris, we specialize in creating unforgettable safari experiences through the world-renowned Kruger National Park and surrounding private reserves. Our passion for Africa’s wildlife, landscapes, and luxury travel drives every journey we create. From private game drives and photographic safaris to luxury lodge stays and tailor-made African adventures, we focus on delivering authentic experiences with exceptional service and attention to detail. Our safaris are designed for travelers seeking more than just a holiday - they are crafted for those wanting to experience the beauty, excitement, and exclusivity of the African wilderness in comfort and style. Whether you are searching for the Big Five, a luxury escape into nature, or a once-in-a-lifetime safari experience, Kruger SA Safaris combines expert local knowledge with personalized hospitality to create journeys that are both seamless and unforgettable.

Why Travel With Us?

  • Luxury & tailor-made safari experiences
  • Private guided safaris
  • Expert local knowledge of Kruger and surrounding reserves
  • Carefully selected lodges and accommodations
  • Personalized service from start to finish
  • Passion for wildlife, and authentic African travel

Our Mission is to provide world-class safari experiences that showcase the beauty of Africa while creating lifelong memories for travelers from around the world.
